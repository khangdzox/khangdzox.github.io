Da Nang City

Da Nang is a beautiful coastal city located in the central of Vietnam. It is well-known for a lot of things, such as amazing sandy beaches, or amazing tourist attractions... However, the things that really make Da Nang famous is its huge number of bridges across the Han River.

City of the Bridges

There is several main bridges in Danang. Down from the north, there are:

  1. Thuan Phuoc Bridge: the highest bridge in Da Nang and longest suspension bridge in Viet Nam, located at the river mouth.
  2. Han River Bridge: the first and only swing bridge in Vietnam, resided near the Administrative Center. It can swing 90 degree at midnight to allow boats to pass through.
  3. Dragon Bridge: the newest bridge in Da Nang, has a steel dragon running along the bridge. It can breathe fire and water every weekends night.
  4. Tran Thi Ly Bridge: the first bridge in Da Nang city, originally built by the French Indochina. It was rebuilt in 2009 and reopened in the same day of the Dragon Bridge in 2013.

Tourist Attractions

Beside bridges, Da Nang also has other places to visit, such as:

  1. My Khe Beach: one of the most beautiful beaches in the world.
  2. Asia Park: an amusement park near city center, usually hold several festivals.
  3. Marble Mountains: magnificient natural landmark in Da Nang.
  4. Ba Na Hills: euro-styled amusement park with a lot of festivals and beautiful structures, including the Golden Bridge.
