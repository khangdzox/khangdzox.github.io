Pentago rules

  • The board is divided into 4 square. Each square has 3x3 holes.
  • Each turn, a player do:
    1. Place a ball in an unoccupied hole
    2. Rotate a square in either direction by 90 degree
  • A player win when there's 5 balls in a row/column/diagonal

Pyramids rules

  • The board is a square with 4x4 holes. Each player has 15 balls in their deck.
  • Each turn, a player do:
    1. Place a ball, either in an unoccupied hole or on other balls
    2. If their balls form a 2x2 square, they have to take 2 out of 4 balls back to their deck.
  • Player whose ball on top of the pyramid win

Tic-Tac-Toe with a twist

There's only 3 Xs and 3 Os. If players run out of their X/O, they have to move one of their X/O on the board.
The first player to get 3 in a row win.
